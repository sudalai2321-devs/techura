About the Event

The Department of Information Technology, School of Engineering and Technology, Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University in Associate with Techtitans proudly presents STRINOX 2K25, a National Level Technical Symposium to ignite innovation and inspire young minds.

A symposium is not just an event but a platform for innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. STRINOX brings together students and problem-solvers from across the nation.

Compile Crew's

A technical coding & debugging challenge.

Harmony Club's

A non-technical contest of creativity, communication & teamwork

STRINOX 2k25 is about learning, exploring, competing, and celebrating knowledge. Join us for this exciting journey of ideas and excellence.

We will provide lunch and snacks for all participants.

Date: 10th September 2025
